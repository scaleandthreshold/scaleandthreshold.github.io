Jake Moore is a conceptual artist based in the Dandenong Ranges, east of Naarm Australia.

Making use of both qualitative and quantitative methods, Jake aims to collapse perceived distinctions between separate subjects, and cleave new fractures in assumed wholes. This has taken form in solo and collaborative work, public talks and private conversations, commissions, gifts, and various group exhibitions and online publications.

Working across drawing and panting, process and instruction, programming, writing, sculpture, composition and sound design, installation, robotics, video, animation and performance. The ultimate goal of which is to raise questions that encourage conversation between different disciplines producing new ways of understanding and revealing the world.
