Moore's Grip (2020)

Performance


This is a simple exercise which can be performed by anyone able to hold their own hand.

Simply clap your fingers together as you norallly would, then reverse the grip, interlocking your fingers in an equal but alternating pattern.

The aim is to reveal an awareness of the seemlingly arbitrary preferences we have for even the most mundane activities.
