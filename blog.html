Blog and Journal

A collection of fragments, potential projects, and miscelaneous interests.

Many projects can take years to develop before turning into something fit for publication or exhibition.

One of the best ways to develop projects is by making regular sketches, taking notes, saving links, and generally playing without the pressure of a final goal in mind. These ideas can be hard to keep track of, but I try to make regular posts here as a way of consolidating resources and inspiration.
