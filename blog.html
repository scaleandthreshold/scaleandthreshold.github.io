Blog and Journal

A collection of fragments, potential projects, and miscelaneous interests.

Many ideas can take years to develop before turning into something fit for publication or exhibition.

One of the best ways to develop ideas is by keep a scap book, making regular sketches, taking notes, saving links, and generally playing without the pressure of a final goal in mind.

These ideas can be hard to keep track of, but by making regular posts and consolidating resources and inspiration, they can come together quite quickly as all the pieces can be easily seen in relation to one another.
